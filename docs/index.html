About Us

i&i Bangladesh is a countrywide organization. We are involved in Import & Export, Trading and Management services, successfully operating from its different locations. We, as a general trading company, source and service domestic customers. The company maintains a large in house data which enables us to quickly source commodities and materials and make prompt and competitive quotations for any customer requirement with the manufacturers information and specification.

It is always our endeavor that we coordinate and deal with reputed manufacturers and factories and that meets the satisfaction in terms of quality as well as price competitiveness of our end user.

Our biggest advantages are based on the abundance of financing resources, broad trade networks, comprehensive market information and our excellent staff.

We have established the wide connection with the business and financial world, and close cooperation has been built up with government bodies, financial institutions, industrial sectors and enterprises.

i&i Bangladesh, a multi diversified general trading company in various, realty, represent many rural manufacturers in the competitive market. These arrangements are an asset to reduce operation cost and become more competitive in the local markets. We take complete responsibility from marketing, taking orders, packing, shipping, documentation and after sales customer support for the rural manufacturers.

The primary objective of our company is Pursuit of excellence in the field of procurement, services and supplies commodities. The organization is consistently endeavoring to achieve optimum results from its various business operations.
